Why Come Here?

Danger!



Yeah, you could go to a normal place and relax.... Or you could
come here, where you will be endlessly thrown in to life
or death threatening situations!

No need to be eased in gently, just walk right up to a herd of
mammoths, they probably won't hurt you! Don't worry about
those giants guarding them I doubt they hit very hard anyway.
Did I also mention we have highly aggressive dragons that
will attack you as you were to transverse the place? Cool as, right?

Nature!



Do you like nature? Great! As you walk around Skyrim you will
quickly learn that nature here doesn't like you

Discover what they mean by 'harsh environment' as you
gleefully freeze in our snowy, cold region

Do you like spiders? Great! We have GIANT ones that will
totally take you on in a fight. You won't even need to ask
them, they will just run right up to you! How cool is that?!
